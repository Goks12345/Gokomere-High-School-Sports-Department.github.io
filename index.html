Welcome

Welcome to Gokomere High School Sports Department! We aim to develop talented athletes, foster teamwork, and promote leadership through sports.

Our programs include football, basketball, athletics, swimming, and more. Every student has an opportunity to participate, improve skills, and compete at regional and national levels.

About Us

Gokomere High School Sports Department has a proud history of nurturing champions. Our mission is to provide students with high-quality sports programs that teach discipline, resilience, and teamwork.

We offer experienced coaching, well-maintained facilities, and regular participation in competitions. Through our programs, students develop athletic skills, character, and leadership qualities.

Overview

Gokomere high school has indeed been the talk of the province and the nation in the area of sport. The institution has produced footballers like Tauya Murehwa, a medical doctor and a national team player and former Dynamos player, Johnson Zimbabe, Ovidy Karuru to mention a few. In the field of chess personalities like Svinurai Chakurira, Kudzanai Mamombe (current grandmaster) have been produced from this institution Each student chooses a sporting activity he/she likes or where he/she does best. After rigorous trials, a student is now selected into the school team by their coaches, based on their competence. Sporting activities/training are done during the week, from 1520 hrs to 1645 hrs daily. For intra-school competitions, the student body is divided into four groups referred to as Houses these are named 1, 2, 3, and 4.
